Driving Hours Rules

This tracker is designed to help drivers comply with UK domestic driving rules as the default setting, ensuring adherence to regulations such as maximum daily driving hours and required breaks as outlined below. In the future, the tracker will feature customizable settings, allowing users to adjust the rules to match different driving regulations or unique operational needs. This flexibility will make it a versatile tool for both domestic and international use, adapting to the diverse requirements of drivers and businesses alike.

Maximum Driving Hours

Drivers must not exceed 10 hours of driving per day, including any overnight trips.

This rule helps prevent driver fatigue and ensures safety for everyone on the road.

Maximum Trip Duration

Drivers must not exceed 5 and 1/2 hours of driving per trip without taking a 30-minute break.

Regular breaks are essential to ensure safety on the road and compliance with legal requirements.

Note: These rules are based on UK domestic driving regulations. Always consult official guidelines for more detailed requirements.
